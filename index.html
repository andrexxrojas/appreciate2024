The APPRECIATE event features research presentations and technology exhibits over two days. Started in 2016, it celebrates groundbreaking student projects from TUP Manila's College of Engineering.

About

For years, the Technological University of the Philippines has always been committed to providing quality education. The educational focus includes engineering and testing , automation, communication, multimedia technology, manufacturing and production engineering, and research and extension services.

APPRECIATE is a two-day research presentation and awards program which covers the advance understanding of higher education, especially through the insights, perspectives and knowledge offered by the methodological research in the field of engineering. It is an event that consists of different research sessions such as Plenary Lectures, Parallel Research Presentation, and Project Booth and Exhibits.

This event is through the initiative of four departments in the College of Engineering (COE) namely: Civil Engineering Department (CE), Electronics Engineering Department (ECE), Electrical Enginerring Department (EE), and Mechanical Engineering Department (ME). Various guests and speakers will be invited as they represent different field and industry sectors necessary for career-building talks for the COE students.

Frequently Asked Questions | Trivia

What is APPRECIATE for?

APPRECIATE stands as a key hub for graduating engineering students, offering them a prime stage to showcase their project research. Not just limited to local exposure, our initiative extends the opportunity for national recognition, appreciating and highlighting the innovative contributions of these aspiring engineers.

Did you know?

During APPRECIATE 2016, we had the honor of being joined by Former Senator Bam Aquino. Hist participation added a significant and insightful dimension to the APPRECIATE 2016 event, enriching the experience for all attendees.

Did you know?

Since its first installment in 2016, APPRECIATE gave a platform to young innovators from TUP - Manila to present their quality research and project studies. Several studies made it to the Philippine Association of Engineering Schools Undergraduate Engineering Design Contest where they bagged awards for their presentation.
